Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)

Royal Challengers Bengaluru is one of the flagship franchises of the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, RCB has built a passionate fanbase known for vocal support and a love of aggressive, entertaining cricket.

History

RCB was formed during the initial IPL franchise auctions in 2008. The team was originally owned by United Spirits (then by Vijay Mallya) and has since gone through several leadership and coaching changes. The franchise attracted marquee players early on, and while it spent many seasons searching for consistency, it produced numerous memorable performances and top-class talents.

Early Years (2008–2014)

RCB's early seasons featured established international players and Indian stars. The team reached the 2009 and 2011 finals, driven by strong batting line-ups. These years established RCB's reputation as a high-scoring team.

Rebuild and Rise (2015–2024)

RCB continued to recruit top talent. The team had fluctuating results but developed a strong core and fan identity. Notable individual seasons and performances kept RCB in spotlight, including several playoff appearances.

2025 Championship

In 2025, RCB achieved a major milestone by winning its first IPL title. The championship was a momentous occasion for the franchise and its fans, and it cemented the team's status among the elite in the IPL.

Stadium

M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru is RCB's home ground. Known for its electric atmosphere and short boundaries, the stadium is a paradise for batters and provides an intense home-ground advantage.

Notable Players (Past and Present)

  • Virat Kohli — One of the most prolific run-scorers in IPL history and a long-time face of the franchise.
  • AB de Villiers — Legendary batter known for his innovation and finishing abilities.
  • Rajat Patidar — Captain (as of 2025), contributed crucial innings in the title-winning campaign.
  • Other notable names: Anil Kumble, Rahul Dravid, Jacques Kallis, Yash Dayal, Bhuvneshwar Kumar.

Achievements

  • IPL Champions: 2025
  • Runners-up: 2009, 2011, 2016
  • Multiple playoff appearances

Fan Culture

RCB fans are among the most passionate in the IPL. Their chants, fan art, and social media presence have made RCB one of the most followed cricket franchises worldwide.
